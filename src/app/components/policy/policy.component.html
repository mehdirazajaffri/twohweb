How can we help?

Delivery:

Our delivery time is between 25 to 30 days. In most cases, you you'd get your products within this time frame.

Delivery time depends on when the brand ships your product to our warehouse in the United States. Most brands deliver the products within 4-9 business days, however, the lead time may vary from brand to brand.

You can check the status of your order by logging into your customer profile and track the order. If you have any other queries, please email us at contact@twohonlinestore.com.

Return:

Returns are NOT ACCEPTED under any circumstances. Once the order is placed, it cannot be changed or returned.

Customers are responsible for communicating the correct sizes/colors of the products that they want to purchase. Two H will not be responsible for any issues pertaining to incorrect size/color of the product communicated by the customers.

Orders once placed cannot be changed or cancelled. If in case the customer wants to cancel the order, the security deposit will not be refunded.
